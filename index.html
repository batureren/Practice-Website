Did you know that Obi-Wan’s Home Planet Is Named after Jon Stewart?

Obi-Wan hails from a planet called Stewjon. It’s never actually appeared in either the canon timeline or the defunct Legends continuity. But it’s canon because George Lucas declared it to be so during an interview with television host Jon Stewart at Celebration V in 2010. Stewjon officially became Obi-Wan’s home planet when it was added to StarWars.com’s Encyclopedia (now Databank) of lore. The original bit — Lucas wryly joking that the planet was called Stewjon in the first iteration of the A New Hope script — is apparently all it took for this mysterious planet to become canon. George Lucas has that power.
